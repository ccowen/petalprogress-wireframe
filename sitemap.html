Sign-up flow — individual vs. admin fork
💡
All sign-up CTAs lead to the same account creation screen. After email/password, the user is asked what brings them here — which forks the onboarding path. Admins are a secondary, quieter option — the default experience is individual.

Marketing nav — active state and link behavior
⚓
The marketing nav has three items: Home, For Teams, and Pricing, plus Sign in and Sign up free. All links navigate directly to their respective pages — no scroll or anchor behavior. Active state is set automatically from the URL: Home on /, For Teams on /teams, Pricing on /pricing.

app.petalprogress.com — logged-in product (separate domain)
💡
The app lives on a completely separate domain from the marketing site. The logged-in nav replaces the marketing nav entirely — Mandala · Gallery · Calendar on the left, avatar menu on the right. Marketing pages (Pricing, Teams, Developers) are accessible via footer or avatar dropdown. Two distinct nav contexts exist: Individual and Admin.
