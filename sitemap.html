app.petalprogress.com ‚Äî logged-in product (separate domain)
üí°
The app lives on a completely separate domain from the marketing site. The logged-in nav replaces the marketing nav entirely ‚Äî Mandala ¬∑ Gallery ¬∑ Calendar on the left, avatar menu on the right. Marketing pages (Pricing, Teams, Developers) are accessible via footer or avatar dropdown. Two distinct nav contexts exist: Individual and Admin.
